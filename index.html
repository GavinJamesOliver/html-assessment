Grow Foods: The Farming Game

You're Growing Lots of Food

In Grow Foods your objective is to grow some food. Lots of food.

Features

Farm the Land:
 - Care for the land and create the world's coolest farm
 - Hoe the ground to prepare it for crops
 -Plant crops in the ground
 -Water the crops with a watering can
 -Fill the watering can at a well
 -Upgrade your tools (This isn't implemented yet but I promise it willl be really cool, you'll just have to take my word for it)
 -Feed and care for various farm animals (Also not yet implemented)

Enjoy High End AAA Graphics:

 -Breathtaking enviorments and nature
 -Photorealistic portraits of characters
